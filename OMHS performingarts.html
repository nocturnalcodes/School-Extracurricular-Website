The performing arts are an exceptional way to teach students leadership skills and confidence through musical knowlegde. The classes students can take include choir, band, orchestra, piano, theater, and dance. Orchestra is divided into to classes; string ensemble and string orchestra. String Orchestra teaches students new techniques and refines basic understanding to encourage students to deepen their understanding. String orchestra pushes those skills to another level and really dives into the confident leadership aspect of performing. The teachers who teach these courses include:
